XOGO

Games have been a popular area of AI research and become a fast growing software industry since 1999. Despite the fast expansion research on intelligent games with learning function is still in its initial phase. Recent research shows that computational intelligence techniques, characterised by numerical learning, optimization and self-organization may provide a powerful toolset to solve the difficulties in game learning. Our web application consists of AI related games like Sudoku, tic tac toe, n-puzzle and snake puzzle. These games are implemented using AI related algorithms like min-max algorithm, a* search algorithm, depth first search algorithm, a* h/2 algorithm and backtracking algorithm .
